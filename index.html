Our Story

A Café Born From
Passion & Community

Founded in 2020 by Rakshita, Brew & Bloom was born from a simple dream — to create a neighbourhood café that felt like home. Every bean is sourced from small farms across Coorg and Chikmagalur, roasted in-house weekly.

🌱
Farm-to-Cup SourcingPartnerships with 6 local estates in Karnataka
👨‍🍳
In-House RoasteryFresh roast every Monday & Thursday
♻️
Zero-Waste KitchenCompostable packaging & local compost pickup
